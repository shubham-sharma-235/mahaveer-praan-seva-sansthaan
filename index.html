Who We Are

Established in 2004, Shree Mahavir Pran Seva Sansthan Trust is an initiative by Dr. Vinod Jain Lohiya, with a vision to spread awareness and expand the aegis of ancient Indian Medical Regime all over the world. Dr. Vinod, provides medical treatment, which he calls “Pran Seva Padhati” and has provided his services all over the world, including India, USA, Dubai etc. The method used in the treatment is a combination of Spiritual Base and Ayurveda, Reiki, Magnet Therapy, Acupressure, Acupuncture, Alternate Medicine, Naturopathy etc. and the treatment is done without any kind of operation or medicines. Dr. Vinod has treated over Lakhs of patients, over a period of 20 years, and provide treatment for all kinds of ailments ranging from Back Pain to Cancer, without prescribing any medicines or operation.

Dr. Vinod believes that in the ancient times, when we had no resources or during the times when medical sector had not developed, our Indian Medical Sciences, through Vedas and Upanishads provided remedies for all such ailments which did not require us to consume any kind of chemical infused medicines or operations, which eventually damages our body. And hence, to give a life to our Indian Medical Regime, he took this initiative by the name of “Shree Mahavir Pran Seva Sansthan Trust” in Jaipur, Rajasthan.

Our Motto And Initiatives

The main objective of this activity is to make people aware that retaining and maintaining their health is in their hands and health deteriorates not because of their age but due to the wrong lifestyle and to encourage them and make them aware of how to live a healthy and happy life, and hence, Dr. Vinod’s encourages everyone to be “Swasth Raho, Mast Raho (Be healthy, Be happy)” and have proper “Aahar and Vihaar (Good Food, Good Lifestyle)”. Our motto is Nar Seva Narayan Seva (Serving Human is Serving God). The Trust aims to make people aware that the cure to every disease lies in their kitchen and there is no need of any kind of medication but only a change in lifestyle. Dr. Vinod organizes at least two camps every month in different areas, to spread this awareness and to help people with their ailments. Till date, the Trust has treated over lakhs of people, including kids, youth and the old. They have organized camps in schools, police camps, NGOs, temples and many other areas. The impact of their treatment has brought about an immense change in the society. People have now begun to trust Ayurveda and believe that every disease has some cure and that can be treated without the use of medicines or by operation, through practicing a disciplined lifestyle and yoga.

Pioneering Holistic Healthcare and Reviving Traditional Medicine

Dr. Vinod Jain Lohiya is a distinguished medical professional known for his expertise in treating chronic illnesses such as cancer, Guillain-Barré syndrome (GBS), diabetes, and Parkinson’s disease. His contributions extend beyond modern medicine, as he actively works to revive and promote the Bharatiya Chikitsa Padhati, ensuring its relevance and accessibility. He champions holistic healthcare through initiatives like Sarva Dharma Sambhaav, integrating traditional and modern healing systems to create inclusive and comprehensive treatment approaches.

Dedication to Social Welfare, Animal Care, and Environmental Conservation

Dr. Lohiya's social contributions include over 25 years of service to orphaned children and vulnerable populations, providing essentials like food, clothing, education, and employment opportunities. His compassion extends to animal welfare through financial aid to Gaushalas and support for cow welfare, ensuring their care and well-being. Additionally, he is deeply committed to environmental conservation, organizing tree-planting drives (focusing on medicinal species), water conservation campaigns, and raising awareness about sustainable living practices.

Community Health Initiatives and Recognition for Outstanding Contributions

His efforts in community health are evident through health camps that bring medical services to underserved areas while educating local volunteers to sustain these practices. Dr. Lohiya is also an advocate for the Fit India Mission, promoting health awareness and fostering holistic well-being.

In recognition of his remarkable contributions, he received the prestigious Karuna Nidhi Award on October 2, 2023, from Shri Sudhanshu Ji Maharaj of the Vishwa Jagriti Mission. His work continues to inspire and highlight the profound impact of his dedication to medicine, social welfare, and environmental stewardship.
